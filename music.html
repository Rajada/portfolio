Steady State

Under my artist name "Steady State" I create soundtracks inspired by games and my own fantasy world-building. I have contributed to other's projects and created works for my own benefit. See some of my highlights below or visit the Steady State bandcamp.

12 Jumps Remaining

Born from a sci-fi world of my own making, 12 Jumps Remaining is a heavily ambient album with a soundtrack feel and a hidden story.

Defend Your Honor

This album is a collection of themed proof-of-concept tracks that are split into scripted subsections. Each track contains sections for situations where ambience, intensity, celebration, or desperation may be required.

LSD Dream Emulator Revamped

As one of several artists contributing to the fan remake of the PS1 game "LSD Dream Emulator", I have created several ambient looping tracks for the project. The tracks reflect the retro, psychedelic, and often disturbing nature of the game.
