Core Mods Puzzles

Under the brand "Core Mods" I design and 3D print a large variety of mechanical puzzles. Challenging and collectable, mechanical puzzles can range from boxes with trick openings to pieces that must be fit in a frame to objects that must be disassembled or assembled in a secret way.
